What is CMS Hosting?
CMS stands for Content Management System, which includes popular tools like Joomla, Drupal, WordPress, etc., so you can deploy your site using any of your preferred CMS which function just as well online as it does in-person with customers.

What is WordPress Hosting?
WordPress is an open-source Content Management System (CMS) / blogging platform that allows the creation of a website in PHP without the need to code. WordPress Hosting is a web hosting product that is optimized for WordPress, usually comes with WordPress pre-installed. Our WordPress Hosting plans are hosted on the Cloud and are designed to deliver speed, optimized security, backups, scalability and automatic updates.

What makes websites on your Hosting Platform faster?
During the deployment of your CMS site on our managed hosting platform, we deploy a unique optimization technology, known as ThunderStack. The ThunderStack technology is a potent combination of the powerful features of Varnish, Memcached, Apache, and Nginx. This unique formula gets you the best-managed CMS hosting that guarantees up to 100% better and fastest page load times.
